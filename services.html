Our Servives

We help you stay healthy with many easy care services.

Consulattion

Meet with our doctors to discuss your health problems, get medical advice, and find the right treatment for your condition.

Outpatient

Get treatment without staying overnight. Come in, see the doctor, get care, and return home the same day.

Inpatient

For serious conditions, we provide full care and monitoring while you stay in our clean, safe, and comfortable hospital rooms.

Laboratory

We perform tests like blood, urine, and other samples to help doctors diagnose and treat your health issues accurately and quickly.

Family Planning

We provide safe family planning methods, information, and support to help you plan and space your children when ready.

Immunization

We offer vaccines for children and adults to protect against common diseases and keep your family safe and healthy.

Minor Surgeries

We perform small, safe surgeries like wound stitching or lump removal in a clean, professional environment with proper care.

Male Circumcision

We offer safe, hygienic circumcision services for infants, children, and adults, done by trained medical professionals.

Marternity

We care for mothers before, during, and after birth with safe delivery, checkups, and support for a healthy pregnancy journey.
